The Instagram algorithm has a special analytical chain for selecting posts that will be more effectively promoted and shown to a wider audience.
Let's see how it works…
  • A user creates a post on Instagram, which can include photos, videos, captions, and hashtags.
  • This post in now visible to their followers and potentially discoverable by others.
  • Instagram’s algorithm analyzes the hashtags used in the post. It assesses hashtag relevance based on post content and user history.
  • It will be featured in the “Explore” section for a wider audience, if the post continues to receive engagement and gains popularity
  • Instagram’s algorithm continues to monitor how well the post is performing with each hashtag. It assesses whether the post is gaining traction within those hashtag communities.
  • Instagram’s algorithm tracks initial post engagement, including likes, comments, shares, and their speed.
  • Soooooooo.....
  • In summary, Instagram's algorithm uses a combination of factors, including initial engagement, hashtag relevance, and user behavior, to determine how to promote posts with the right hashtags.
